Hello! Welcome to my Challenge Blog posts!


Why calling it as "Challenge Blog"?

If you know me, you know I have big love for audio, music, and programming. I have been programming apps, making beats, writing songs as hobbies. Now when I look back, I wish that I did make a space to show things that I built in my freetime, so that everyone can use them or I could just store as a personal hobby portfolio.

Better late than never! Well... technically there is no deadline, so it is not really that late hah?!
I create this blog page to track my progress and make my hobby more "organized". And this is also a way to challenge myself to always create!


What exactly is the "challenge"?

Each month I will pick a theme and challenge myself on creating 4 items related to that specific topic.
This could range from beatmaking, sound designing to programming apps (eg. create 4 plugins in a month) Basically, anything! To be practical, if on that month, I am busy, I might skip on the challenge part. But I will commit myself into doing these at least 3 times (3 months). Why number 4? 4 means that I need to build 1 thing per week which I hope is a practical challenge to myself!
